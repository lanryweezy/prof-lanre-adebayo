Academic Profile

Professor Sulaiman Olanrewaju Adebayo is a distinguished academic in the field of Psychology with 39 years of experience in teaching, research, and practice. He has been a Professor of Psychology and Behavioural Studies since 2007 and is currently affiliated with Ekiti State University.

Throughout his extensive career at the university, he has held several key leadership positions, including Head of the Department of Psychology, Dean of the Faculty of The Social Sciences, and Director of the Institute of Peace and Security Studies.

He is a highly respected figure in his field, recognized as a fellow of both the Nigerian Psychological Association (NPA) and the Nigerian Association of Social Psychologists (NASP). He currently serves as the President of NASP, a role that underscores his leadership and commitment to the advancement of social psychology in Nigeria.

Prof. Adebayo also serves as the Editor-in-Chief of the Nigeria Journal of Social Psychology (NJSP), contributing significantly to the dissemination of knowledge in his field.

Research Interests

Social Psychology

Examines social behavior, morality, intergroup relations, and attitudes within various cultural contexts.

Organizational Psychology

Focuses on job stress, supervisory behavior, and their impact on job satisfaction and organizational dynamics.

Health Psychology

Investigates psychological factors related to health, illness, and healthcare systems.
